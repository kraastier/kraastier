About Us

A brand that originates from India- transpiring as unorthodox fashion and culturally defining street style movements. K’RAAS TIER is a women’s fast fashion brand with collections that epitomize the fashionable and ornate lifestyle of today’s trendsetting women. This is a brand of choice for the fashion-conscious, independent young women who want to dress well and classic. K’RAAS TIER offers quality, affordable and influencing clothing for the young and stylish woman who wants to have fun with her look.

Dress well & classic

Launched in 2021, K’RAAS TIER covers a wide range of trendy casual wear, relaxed vacation wear, party dresses, shirts, blouses, alongside essential wardrobe staples, at desirable prices, developing a modern and empowering style of clothing for women.

The brand’s products are inspired from trends around the world- global runway and fast fashion trends that have found favor in India and Internationally.

The brand caters to every aspect of a woman’s clothing needs, be it corporate, casual, club or ceremonial, without compromising on ethics and quality.

Return and Exchange policy

Please inspect your order upon reception and contact us immediately if the merchandise is defective, damaged or if you received the wrong item. We have a 48- HOUR exchange policy, which means you have 48 hours after receiving your item to request an exchange. To be eligible for an exchange, your merchandise must be in the same condition that you received it, unused, with tags intact and in its original packaging. You are eligible for refunds or returns only if the merchandise is defective or damaged. We do not accept any damage complaints after 48 hours of shipment. You can contact us at- kraastier@gmail.com or WhatsApp us on +91-7009041556 or DM us on Instagram with your order number. We will notify you once we have received and inspected your return and the order amount will be refunded into your account. You have to ship back the merchandise to our warehouse (shipping details will be shared once return request is initiated). You are eligible for an exchange if you have received the wrong size. You can only change the size of your merchandise. We do not offer merchandise exchange or return on orders shipped internationally. You have to ship back the merchandise to our warehouse (shipping details will be shared once exchange request is initiated) and we will dispatch the exchange item as a replacement. We will not be liable to pay any taxes/charges arising due to return or exchange shipment.
