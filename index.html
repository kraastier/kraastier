About Us

A brand that originates from India- transpiring as unorthodox fashion and culturally defining street style movements. K’RAAS TIER is a women’s fast fashion brand with collections that epitomize the fashionable and ornate lifestyle of today’s trendsetting women. This is a brand of choice for the fashion-conscious, independent young women who want to dress well and classic. K’RAAS TIER offers quality, affordable and influencing clothing for the young and stylish woman who wants to have fun with her look.

Dress well & classic

Launched in 2021, K’RAAS TIER covers a wide range of trendy casual wear, relaxed vacation wear, party dresses, shirts, blouses, alongside essential wardrobe staples, at desirable prices, developing a modern and empowering style of clothing for women.

The brand’s products are inspired from trends around the world- global runway and fast fashion trends that have found favor in India and Internationally.

The brand caters to every aspect of a woman’s clothing needs, be it corporate, casual, club or ceremonial, without compromising on ethics and quality.

Return Policy

We are happy to accept returns or exchanges of any unworn, regularly priced merchandise for up to 7 days after purchase. For shipments, the customer is responsible for covering the cost of a return. Discounted/sale items are not eligible for return. Kindly ensure to send the images at least 3 for us to analyse the condition of the product. Return/exchange request placed without the images shall not be entertained. Also, if any exchange or return request is made post 7 days from the date of delivery, the system will not accept the request. All items to be returned or exchanged must be unused and in their original condition with all original tags and packaging intact. Under exchange policy, only size changes are allowed. In case, you have purchased an item which has a free gift/offer associated with it and you wish to return the main item, then you will have to return the free product as well. Unused and unaltered products maybe returned for a refund or exchange in original K’RAAS TIER packaging. Refunds will be processed within 7 days from the date of returned/exchanged items being received in our warehouse. Any shipping charges incurred at the time of placing the order will not be refunded.
